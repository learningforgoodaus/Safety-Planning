Select a heading to understand the survivor's reality

The Power Gap

A perpetrator often controls the "logistics" of life—money, phones, and transportation—meaning the survivor may have no immediate access to the tools they need to leave.

The Emotional Load

The person is likely in a state of high cortisol (stress), making logical, long-term thinking difficult. Your calm, non-judgmental presence acts as their first "safe space".

Leaving is the Most Dangerous Time

Separation is the period of highest risk for lethal violence. A safety plan must be discreetly managed so it does not trigger an escalation if discovered by the perpetrator.
